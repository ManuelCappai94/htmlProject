Devil May Cry 3

Game Details
  • Release year: 2005
  • Genre: Action-adventure / Hack and slash
  • Platforms : Playstation 2, Steam, PS3, Xbox 360, PS4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch
  • Developer : Capcom

Why Is This Game So Amazing?

Maybe it is because of the extreme difficulty, or maybe because the characters, boss fights, and weapons are so well designed.

But no.

Dante is an over-the-top protagonist, and Vergil is an over-the-top antagonist.
This duo is one of the best you can find.
And the combat is incredible, (after Devil May Cry 5 and Bayonetta):
endless and stylish combos that reward execution.

The story is simple, but surprisingly deep, and on song in particular perfectly matches its dramatic and stylish tone:
Devils Never Cry, a song that captures the soul of the game and the conflict between Dante and Vergil.

It is a unique experience.
